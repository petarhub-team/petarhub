What is PETAR Hub?

PETAR Hub is a research team dedicated to advancing technology in areas like Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, IoT, and Embedded Systems. We bring together students, scholars, and professionals to collaborate on cutting-edge projects.

Who can join PETAR Hub?

We welcome undergraduate and graduate students, researchers, and professionals with a strong interest in technology and innovation. Applicants should be enthusiastic about collaborating on research and projects.

What benefits do members receive?

Members gain hands-on experience in research, access to cutting-edge tools, and the opportunity to collaborate on impactful projects. You'll also build your professional network and enhance your skills in the latest technological advancements.

How do I apply?

Simply fill out the interest form using the link provided. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you regarding the next steps.
